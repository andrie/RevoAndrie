Hallo World

Every person has a different learning style. I learn best by experimenting and doing, frequently by writing R code. However, I frequently forget what I’ve done or how to do it. And sometimes I just want to share my ideas with others. My intention with this blog is to write down random ideas on a variety of things I work on.
